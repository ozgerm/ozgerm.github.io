About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Aalborg University and a Researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology. My work focuses on future telecommunication systems and machine learning for engineering, with expertise across wireless and networked systems, 5G/6G and non-terrestrial networks, AI-native network management, mobility management, localisation, and engineering decision support.

A recurring theme in my research is the use of information-theoretic reasoning to guide the design of learning methods: how channel structure, coding principles, and measures of informativeness can improve model selection, dataset design, and reliable inference in safety-critical and resource-constrained engineering environments.
